Transport

Getting around Berlin is easy! The S-Bahn can take you anywhere in central Berlin, and at a reasonable price. There are 3 different zones: A, B and C and the price varies according to which zone you want to go to. If you’re aged between 6 to 14, you are eligible for the reduced price. **Once you get your ticket, you have to VALIDATE IT, BUT there are no barriers when you go on the train. HOWEVER, if you are caught without a ticket, you will get fined up to €200! (The inspectors have been known not to wear a uniform so BEWARE!!!)

Student Hacks

  • If the weather is nice, go to the Tempelhofer Park and bring a picnic basket, you’ll get a great snapshot of Berlin life.
  • The East Side Gallery is free and in an artsy part of Berlin, this is definitely a must-see (plus, you’ll get some instagram-worthy pictures).
  • Sandeman tours is very popular in Berlin and these are free as long as you book a slot in advance so be sure to do that! You might even meet other like-minded travellers on the tours to go on adventures with.
